Description

What is a Chocolate Chip Cookie? A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal or raisins as well.

Ingredients

  • Two sticks salted butter
  • Two cups sugar
  • Tablespoon molasses
  • Two eggs
  • Tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Two teaspoons salt
  • Teaspoon baking soda
  • Two-and-a-half cups bread flour
  • 12-ounce bag chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt the butter completely.
  3. Mix in sugar, molasses, eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Mix in baking soda and two cups of flour.
  5. Add flour until dough appears wet but is not terribly sticky.
  6. Refridgerate dough for at least 30 min.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
